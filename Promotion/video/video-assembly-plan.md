# Video Assembly Plan

## Inputs

- slide images exported from final deck
- audio files generated from `Promotion/audio/tts-plan.md`

## Assembly Steps

1. Export each slide to PNG
2. Generate one narration track per slide
3. Build one MP4 segment per slide with still image plus narration
4. Concatenate all segments into final talk video

## Narrative Scope

- short conference-style walkthrough
- emphasize workflow, evaluation setup, and main safety result

## Constraints

- preserve simulation-only framing
- no product-demo claims
